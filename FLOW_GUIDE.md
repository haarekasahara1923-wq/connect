# InfluencerConnect: How it Works

Direct link to the implementation plan: [/how-it-works](/how-it-works)

## Summary of the Platform Flow

### 1. Registration
- **Influencer**: Choose roles and build a profile showcasing your reach.
- **Brand**: Join and start looking for local creators for your product/service.

### 2. Login & Dashboards
- **Automatic Redirection**: When you log in, you will be automatically redirected to your appropriate dashboard.
- **Influencer Dashboard**: Manage requests, earnings, and profile.
- **Brand Dashboard**: Track bookings, budget, and chat with creators.

### 3. Campaign Lifecycle
1. **Search**: Find influencers in Tier 2/3 cities of India.
2. **Book**: Secure payment via escrow (Razorpay).
3. **Execute**: Direct communication and content delivery within the app.
4. **Complete**: Verify content and release payment.

## Functional Directories
- `/app/influencer`: The core influencer experience.
- `/app/brand`: The merchant/business owner interface.
- `/app/(public)`: Marketplace discovery pages.
